About the Event

A Celebration of Cinema & Song

Anvesha is IIIT Kalyani's grandest Bollywood extravaganza — a celebration of melody, movement, drama, and the timeless magic of Hindi cinema. From soul-stirring performances to electrifying dance acts, this is the stage where your inner star steps into the spotlight.

Dress up, show up, and let the Bollywood fever take over!
